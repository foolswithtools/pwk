# Work Principles

> These principles govern how GPWK commands behave. Edit to match your work style.

## Time Management

### Deep Work Windows
- **Minimum block**: 2 hours
- **Preferred time**: Morning (9:00-12:00)
- **Max per day**: 2 blocks

### Meeting Boundaries
- **No meetings before**: 10:00 AM
- **No meetings after**: 4:00 PM
- **Buffer between meetings**: 15 minutes

### Breaks
- **Pomodoro style**: 25 min work, 5 min break
- **Long break**: 15-30 min after 4 pomodoros
- **Recovery time**: 10 min break every 90 min of deep work

## Task Management

### Daily Limits
- **Maximum significant tasks**: 6
- **Maximum total tasks**: 10
- **Quick wins don't count**: Tasks under 15 min

### Carryover Rules
- **c1 threshold**: Normal, just track
- **c2 threshold**: Warning, consider blockers
- **c3 threshold**: Mandatory breakdown consideration

### Two-Minute Rule
- If a task takes less than 2 minutes, do it now
- Don't add to backlog or schedule

## AI Delegation

### Safe to Delegate
- Research and information gathering
- Summarization and synthesis
- First drafts of documentation
- Boilerplate code generation
- Test case generation
- Data formatting and cleanup

### Keep Personal
- Decisions with significant impact
- Relationship-dependent communication
- Creative direction and vision
- Anything requiring institutional knowledge
- Final reviews and approvals

### Delegation Review
- Always review AI results before acting on them
- AI work products need human polish

## Energy Management

### Task-Energy Matching
- **Morning** (high energy): Deep work, creative tasks, hard problems
- **Afternoon** (medium energy): Meetings, collaboration, reviews
- **Late day** (lower energy): Admin, quick wins, planning

### Energy Signals
- Use `~deep` for tasks requiring focus
- Use `~shallow` for low-cognitive tasks
- Use `~quick` for under-15-minute tasks

## Weekly Rhythm

### Monday
- Weekly planning
- Clear inbox/triage backlog

### Friday
- Weekly review
- Close out loose ends
- Prepare for next week

### Sunday (Optional)
- Light weekly preview
- Set intentions

## Review Rituals

### Daily Shutdown
- Run `/gpwk.review` before ending work
- Clear Today column or apply carryover
- Set tomorrow's top priority

### Weekly Review
- Review completion rates
- Identify stuck items
- Adjust principles if needed
