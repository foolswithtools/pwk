# Personal Work Principles

> These principles govern how I work. They are referenced by all /pwk.* commands.
> Review and customize these to match your work style.

---

## Time Management

### P1: Deep Work Windows
Protect focused time blocks for complex work.
- **Rule**: 2-hour minimum blocks, no interruptions
- **Application**: Morning hours (8-10 AM) reserved for deep work
- **Used by**: /pwk.plan

### P2: Two-Minute Rule
Handle quick tasks immediately.
- **Rule**: If it takes <2 minutes, do it now
- **Application**: Don't add to task list, just execute
- **Used by**: /pwk.capture

### P3: Meeting Boundaries
Control meeting sprawl.
- **Rule**: No meetings before 10 AM, limit to 4 hours/day
- **Application**: Protect morning focus time
- **Used by**: /pwk.plan

---

## Task Management

### P4: Daily Task Limit
Prevent overcommitment.
- **Rule**: Maximum 6 significant tasks per day
- **Application**: Excess moves to tomorrow
- **Used by**: /pwk.plan

### P5: Carryover Threshold
Address chronic incomplete tasks.
- **Rule**: Task carried 3+ times must be addressed
- **Application**: Breakdown, delegate, drop, or calendar-block
- **Used by**: /pwk.carryover

### P6: One Thing at a Time
Focus on single tasks.
- **Rule**: Work on one task until done or blocked
- **Application**: No context-switching mid-task
- **Used by**: /pwk.log

---

## AI Delegation

### P7: Delegation Criteria
Know what to delegate.
- **Delegate**: Research, drafts, summaries, boilerplate, data gathering
- **Keep Personal**: Relationships, decisions, creative direction, judgment calls
- **Used by**: /pwk.delegate

### P8: Review AI Output
AI assists, doesn't replace.
- **Rule**: Always review AI-generated content
- **Application**: Treat AI output as draft, not final
- **Used by**: /pwk.delegate

---

## Energy Management

### P9: Energy-Task Matching
Align work with energy levels.
- **High Energy**: Creative work, complex problems, writing
- **Low Energy**: Admin, email, routine tasks
- **Used by**: /pwk.plan

### P10: Recovery Time
Build in breaks.
- **Rule**: 10-minute break every 90 minutes
- **Application**: Step away, move, hydrate
- **Used by**: /pwk.plan

---

## Capture & Process

### P11: Capture Everything
Don't trust memory.
- **Rule**: If it's important, write it down
- **Application**: Use /pwk.capture liberally
- **Used by**: /pwk.capture

### P12: Process Daily
Don't let inbox grow.
- **Rule**: Process inbox to zero each day
- **Application**: End-of-day processing ritual
- **Used by**: /pwk.review

---

## Shutdown Ritual

### P13: Daily Review
Close the day properly.
- **Rule**: Always run /pwk.review before stopping
- **Application**: Clears mental load, prepares tomorrow
- **Used by**: /pwk.review

### P14: Weekly Review
Maintain the system.
- **Rule**: Sunday evening review of week
- **Application**: Review principles, plan next week
- **Used by**: /pwk.plan week
